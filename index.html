3 categories of events

Gilmour Speech and Debate features original speeches, fast-paced debates, and expressive performances. Students grow as thinkers, speakers, and leaders.

Debate

Debate events build confident speakers and sharp thinkers. Students learn to argue clearly and respectfully. Every practice and competition helps them grow.

Speech

Gilmour Speech events build confident communicators. Students deliver original speeches and respond to current issues. Each event strengthens clarity and persuasive skill.

Interp

Interp events focus on performance, emotion, and storytelling. Students bring literature to life through dramatic, humorous, or poetic pieces. Each event builds confidence, creativity, and stage presence.
